==========
Beyond RAM
==========


This is the last module. We have covered a lot of ground and we now
take a step back. We have based everything on random access machines,
but other approaches are possibles: Parallel computer, quantum
computer, Biological computer, AI, etc. We take a quick look at
parallel and quantum computing and see where they fit.
